SCOPE

Small Claims Court

The Small Claims Court is limited to subject matters whose value is below KES. 1, 000, 000. The Kisumu Small Claims Court is only authorised to conduct claims over matters of Kisumu residents or matters whose cause of action arose within Kisumu.

Civil Law

compensation for personal injuries

Contract Law

contracts for sale and supply of goods or services; contracts relating to money held and received; and set-off and counterclaim under any contract.

Tort Law

liability in tort in respect of loss or damage caused to any property or for the delivery or recovery of movable property
